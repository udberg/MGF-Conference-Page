MISK GLOBAL FORUM 2020 CONCLUDES WITH CALL FOR ‘GENERATION SOLVE’ TO DEFINE THEIR POTENTIAL

2020.11.12(TUE) ~ 14(THU)

Main Program

Getting Started

Misk Global Forum brings young leaders, creators and thinkers together with established global innovators to explore, experience and experiment with ways to meet the challenge of change.

Lectures

Speakers from all nations of the world will deliver addresses on the actions required to effectively combat the issues faced by the younger generation on entreprenuership.

Forums

Open forums will be held to collectively discuss proposed solutions and inform young people around the world on the benefits and challenges of entreprenuership.

Plenaries

Observe the plenaries as experienced entreprenuers and world leaders take on questions from the audience.

Network Sessions

Social and networking events will take place to allow attendees to meet others who share their vision for the future.

Featured Speakers

Mr. Prince EA.

Spoken Word Artist, Poet, Rapper & Filmaker

Richard Williams, better known by the stage name Prince Ea, is an American spoken word artist, poet, rapper, and filmmaker. Prince Ea initially pursued a career as a hip-hop artist. Inspired by artists like Immortal Technique and Canibus, he started and popularized the “Make S.M.A.R.T. Cool” movement to promote values like intelligence, free thought, unity, and creativity in hip hop music and culture.

Eng. Ahmad M. Al Ghamdi

People VP
STC Group

Ahmad has over 15 years of experience in the field of Human Resources and Business Management in general. He has worked in many industries including Retail, Aviation, FMCG, Manufacturing, Health Care, Consulting & Communication.

Ms. Nora AlNashwan

Consultant
IBM Services

Nora is a Consultant specializing in Blockchain technology at IBM. Prior to IBM, Nora worked on building innovative digital solutions for several companies, including startups and enterprises. She also worked on advocating developers to inspire and equip them with the knowledge they need.

H.E. Sim Ann

Senior Minister of State, Ministry of Communications and Information and Ministry of Culture, Community and Youth
Parliament of Singapore

Sim Ann is currently the Senior Minister of State for the Ministry of Communications and Information and the Ministry of Culture, Community and Youth. She is also a Member of Parliament for the Holland-Bukit Timah GRC.

Mr. Guillaume Benech

Chairman
Odace Media

Mr. Benech founded the cultural magazine L’Petit Mardi at the age of 12. For this initiative, the first magazine to be entirely written by young people and aimed at young people, Mr. Benech received an award from Google France. Later, Guillaume Benech transformed the company into Odace Media, a communications consulting firm.

Mr. Riad Hamade

Executive Editor for the Middle East and North Africa
Bloomberg News

Mr. Hamade is Bloomberg News’ Executive Editor for the Middle East and North Africa, leading the company’s coverage in some of the most important emerging economies.
